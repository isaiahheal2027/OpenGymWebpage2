Basketball

Basketball is a 5v5 team game of 4 12 minute quarters with no score limit, whoever scores the most points within the tine limit provided wins the game

RULES

https://www.ducksters.com/sports/basketballrules.php

The player must bounce, or dribble, the ball with one hand while moving both feet. If, at any time, both hands touch the ball or the player stops dribbling, the player must only move one foot. The foot that is stationary is called the pivot foot.The basketball player can only take one turn at dribbling. In other words, once a player has stopped dribbling they cannot start another dribble. A player who starts dribbling again is called for a double-dribbling violation and looses the basketball to the other team. A player can only start another dribble after another player from either team touches or gains control of the basketball. This is usually after a shot or pass.The ball must stay in bounds. If the offensive team looses the ball out of bounds the other team gets control of the basketball.

Football

a game that is played by two teams of eleven players using an oval-shaped ball with 4 15 minute quarters. Players try to score points by passing or carrying the ball to their opponents' end of the field, or by kicking it over a bar fixed between two posts.

RULES

https://www.realbuzz.com/articles-interests/sports-activities/article/basic-american-football-rules/

Each team has separate teams for separate sides, they have a defensive team and a offensive team, the offensive team has 4 chances of advancing the ball 10 yards, and if the offensive team advances those 10 yards then the count resets and they get 4 more chances until they score a touchdown or get stopped by the defense. If they get stopped by the defense then they flip sides and the defensive team gets off the field and let the offense play and the offensive team gets off so the defense can play. Touchdown(6 points) Extra point is the kick after the touchdown which is worth 1 point, A safety is 2 points the defensive team can get if they tackle an offensive player on their own touchdown

Soccer

a game played on a field between two teams of 11 players with two 45 mintue halfs each with the object to propel a round ball into the opponent's goal by kicking or by hitting it with any part of the body except the hands and arms

RULES

https://www.soccer.com/guide/rules-of-soccer-guide

Soccer players are allowed to advance the ball with any part of there body except for the hands and arms, the only player that can use there hand is the goalkeeper

Golf

a game played on a large open-air course, in which a small hard ball is struck with a club into a series of small holes in the ground, the object being to use the fewest possible strokes to complete the course.

RULES

https://www.golfsidekick.com/knowledge/basic-rules-of-golf-for-beginners/

You must tee the ball up behind the line created by the tee markers, if you take a swing and you miss it still counts as a swing, always play the ball where it lies you can't kick it or move it to a better spot.Double bogey or higher= no points, Bogey=1 point, par=2 points birdie=3 points eagle= 4 points Albatross=5 Points. A hole in one occurs when a ball hit from a tee lands in the hole.

Baseball

a game played with a bat and ball by two teams of nine players each on a field with four bases that mark the course a runner must take to score

RULES

https://www.sportsengine.com/baseball/basic-rules-baseball

Players score runs by hitting the ball and running around the four bases (running in a counterclockwise direction) before a player in the field is able to get them out. This can be done in stages or all in one go, depending on how many bases the player thinks they can reach without getting out. If a player reaches the base before the ball, they are called safe.Once a batter has hit the ball, then they are referred to as a runner.Runners try to reach the bases, and can remain safe on a base until the next hitter comes.If the ball is caught direct from the batters hit, then that batter is out. The same applies if the ball is thrown to first base before the batter arrives at the base.A batter is out if he or she fails to connect with the ball three times after three good pitches which is often referred to as being out on strikes.The offensive player can also be out by a tagout when a defensive player touches the runner with the ball or a glove with the ball in.After the batting team has three players out, the teams switch so that the other side does the fielding while the other bats.The defensive team position a catcher directly behind the batter who catches the ball if it is not hit.

Tennis

an indoor or outdoor game that is played with rackets and a light elastic ball by two players or pairs of players on a level court (as of clay or grass) divided by a low net

RULES

https://olympics.com/en/news/tennis-rules-regulations-how-to-play-basics

The serving player has to stand behind the baseline of his side of the tennis court and within the bounds of the centre mark and the sideline.For a successful serve, the server has to toss the ball up with his non-playing hand and hit it with the racket before it bounces. The ball needs to cross the net and land within the serving area which is marked on the diagonally opposite side of the court for it to be deemed a legal serve.Each player is allowed two serves per point. If a player hits the net, or the balls first bounce occurs outside the serving area, it's called a service fault and the server has a second serve to initiate the point.If the servers foot touches the baseline or goes out of bounds of the sideline, it is known as a foot fault and the server will have a second serve.If a player commits a fault on his second serve also, it is called a double fault and the receiving player gets that point.However, if a players serve hits the net and lands within the serving area, then the server will still have two serves to initiate the point. This situation is known as let.The server has to alternate between the vertical halves of the tennis court for each point.If the server manages to land a legal serve and the receiver is unable to return the ball, it is known as an ace and the server gets the point.Any point is initiated by the server and it is the job of the receiver to return it successfully.Once the serve is returned, the two players engage in a rally, i.e trading shots back-and-forth over the net and within the sidelines and baseline.To win a point, a player must hit a shot either before the ball has bounced or after the first bounce. If the ball bounces twice, then the player with the last shot wins the point.
